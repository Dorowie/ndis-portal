2. Data Privacy & AI Communications

We prioritize the privacy of your sensitive information.

  • Non-Recording of Conversations: We do not record or store the transcripts of your live interactions with the chatbot. Once a session is closed, the specific conversational flow is purged from our active records.
  • Data Storage: Personal data required for account management and booking (such as names, contact details, and booking history) is stored using industry-standard encryption and security protocols.
  • Purpose of Use: Data is used strictly for the functional purpose of managing your bookings and improving the user experience. We do not sell user data to third parties.
